About me

My broad research interests include combustion chemistry, chemical kinetic models, and detailed and reduced mechanism development. My current research focuses on experiments on chemical kinetics using shock tubes and rapid compression machine. Previously, I have worked with Prof. Ming Jia on chemical mechanism. Currently, I am advised by Prof. Aamir Farooq.
